About Me

Hi there! I'm a tech-savvy problem-solver with a unique background in Business English, Marketing, Economics, and Culture Studies. I've always had a passion for computer science and it was this passion that inspired me to teach myself how to code. My first project, the "Movie Recommendation Engine" web app, was a hit among my friends who used it to pick the best Netflix shows for their weekends. I was thrilled to see how my skills could make a real difference in people's lives.

One of my biggest career highlights so far was during my co-op in the fourth semester when I built an entire application system from scratch for a medium-sized company accross countries. The system helped HR to maintain the vacation days for hundreds of employees and was complete with a friendly UI and a database on the back-end. It was a challenging and rewarding experience that showcased my technical abilities and problem-solving skills.

I love building things that people can use and enjoy, and I have a special fondness for automating repetitive tasks. With my hands-on technical expertise and experience as a full-stack developer, I'm on a mission to design, develop, and implement solutions that make a real difference. My goal is to create applications that streamline everyday tasks, freeing up time for people to spend on what really matters (your family, your loved-one, your furry friends, your friends, etc). So, let's work together to bring your ideas to life and make technology work for us!"
